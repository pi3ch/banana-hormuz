HOW TO PLAY

Two fighters stand on the cliffs across the Strait of Hormuz. Take turns lobbing your signature projectile at your opponent.

Set the ANGLE (0–90°) and POWER (0–100), then FIRE! Mind the WIND — the arrow shows its direction & strength.

Each fighter throws their own weapon: Donald a Truth Social tweet, Bibi a missile, Khamenei a Shahed drone.

First to 3 round wins takes the match. Win fast (fewer shots) for a higher score!

Keys: ← → adjust angle · ↓ ↑ adjust power · SPACE/ENTER fire
